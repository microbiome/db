**The research team of Dr Leo Lahti is focusing on modern statistical data analysis, with key applications in large population cohort studies, microbial ecology of the human body, and computational history.**

Research topics
----------------
- Theory: Statistical machine learning/AI, Computational science, High-throughput data analysis  
- Methods: Probabilistic programming, Open research software, Open science
- Applications: Microbial ecology of the human body, Functional genomics, Biomedicine, Computational social science, Computational history
